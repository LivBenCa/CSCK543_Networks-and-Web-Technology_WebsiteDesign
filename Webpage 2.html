Park Layout

If you would ask us what the Whiteside Snowboard Park makes so special, we would probably all agree that it is the simplicity and DIY style. Since we were only kids at that time, it was just a simple meadow with a slope and some obsticles - it was our own work that we knew in every detail.

The ease also came with the fact that there was nothing much between you and the fun. No tickets to buy, no lift to use, and no journey upfront. The slope starts right next to the street and the parking space. You could sit on the lid handle of your trunk and start straight from there.

After the starting point we mostly built rails or other stuff to slide on - the longer, the better. Often we installed two rails right behind each other, with only a few metres for jumps, gaps or spins.

To keep the action up until the end, we always installed a quarter pipe at the foot of the slope and use the speed make some incredible plants or jumps.
